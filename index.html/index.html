How We Work
Our Monthly Process
1

Strategy & Planning (Week 1)

Trend research, competitor analysis, content calendar planning, hook & script ideation for the month ahead.

2

Production (Weeks 1–3)

Shooting days executed — iPhone or cinematic camera depending on package. On-site direction, lighting, and composition handled by our team.

3

Post-Production (Weeks 2–4)

Editing, colour grading, sound design (Pkg 02), caption writing, hashtag mapping, and carousel photo retouching.

4

Approval & Scheduling

Content shared for review. Once approved, all posts are scheduled and published per the agreed calendar.

5

Report & Optimise (End of Month)

Monthly analytics report with performance insights, top-content analysis, and recommendations for the next cycle.

Please note: Travel and accommodation charges are applicable separately. Meta Ads, SEO and influencer collaborations are not included in either package and can be scoped separately upon request.
